How It Works

Three Steps. Infinite Characters.

Talkie is built around three core pillars that take you from idea to a living, talking AI personality.

🧱

Build

Design your avatar's identity, personality, voice, and backstory. Choose from preset characters or craft something entirely unique — give it a name, a world, and a reason to exist.

💬

Talk

Watch your avatar come to life in real conversations. Play and learn together with mini-games like charades, trivia, and drawing. Share interactions, debate, and connect.

✨

Customize

Earn and unlock outfits, hairstyles, and accessories. Friendship is power — the more you interact, the more your avatar evolves alongside you.

For Consumers

Your imagination, animated.

Design avatars of friends, family, or entirely invented characters — then watch them hold real conversations, react, and grow. Every interaction becomes shareable social content.

  • Play mini-games together: trivia, charades, drawing

  • Unlock premium characters and customizations
  • Use avatars to practice a language or learn a subject

Brand-embodied AI at scale.
