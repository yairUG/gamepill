How It Works

Three Pillars. Infinite Characters.

Every Talkie is built around three core capabilities — from crafting the identity to real-time conversation.

🧱

Build

Design your avatar's identity, voice, personality, and knowledge base. Train it on your product, brand story, or domain expertise — with Game Pill's studio team handling the high-fidelity character art and animation.

💬

Talk

Deploy a character that holds real, unscripted conversations in real time. Sub-second voice responses, natural dialogue flow, and the ability to answer questions, teach concepts, or represent your brand with precision.

✨

Customize

White-label the experience for your platform. Define safety boundaries, brand guardrails, and conversation scope. Measure engagement with privacy-safe analytics that respect your users' data.
